A CALL TO LIVE

Breathing is not living. Life is not really about inhaling and exhaling, its beyond the pulse and physical beating of your heart. Until you know your reason for being you don't start living. You need to know who you are and why you are!

Life can be not only beautiful but also perfect. Perfection is not an unattainable pursuit! Happiness can be realised; it is can be more than a goal. You begin to live when you find satisfaction, and fullfilment.
